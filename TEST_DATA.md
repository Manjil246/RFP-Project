# RFP Test Data

## 1. RFP Natural Language Description

```
Hi, we're setting up a new development office and need to purchase some IT equipment. Our budget is around $45,000 for everything. We need this delivered within 4 weeks from order confirmation.

We're looking for:
- 25 laptops (preferably Dell or HP, minimum 16GB RAM, 512GB SSD, Intel i7 or equivalent)
- 20 monitors (27-inch, 4K resolution preferred, but 2K is acceptable)
- 5 docking stations compatible with the laptops
- All necessary cables and accessories

Payment terms: We prefer net 30 days, but can discuss other options. We need at least 2 years warranty on laptops and 1 year on monitors. Also, we'd like to know if you can provide on-site setup and configuration support.

Please include pricing breakdown for each item, delivery timeline, and any bulk discounts you can offer. Looking forward to your proposal.
```

## Summary of Differences:

**Manjil's Proposal:**
- Lower total price: $38,684 (within budget)
- Faster delivery: 3 weeks
- Better warranty: 3 years on laptops, 2 years on monitors
- Free on-site setup included
- QHD monitors (2K) instead of 4K
- More detailed and professional formatting

**Prashanna's Proposal:**
- Higher total price: $44,285 (slightly over budget)
- Slower delivery: 25-28 business days (closer to deadline)
- Standard warranty: 2 years on laptops, 1 year on monitors
- Setup service costs extra: $500
- 4K monitors (meets preferred spec)
- Less detailed but still complete

These differences will create an interesting comparison scenario where Manjil offers better value and terms, but Prashanna offers higher-spec monitors (4K vs 2K).
